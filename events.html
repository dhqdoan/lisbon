Thursday, August 7

Sunset River Cruise

All aboard for a sunset cruise to kick off our wedding festivities! Dinner and drinks will be served.

Attire: Cocktail Casual (think summer dresses, boat attire, linen, polos, flat sandals, etc.)

6:00pm Meet promptly at the first dock near Estação Fluvial Sul and Sueste, just southeast of Praça do Comércio. It is approximately a 10–20 minute walk from the hotels or a quick Uber.
6:25pm The boat departs. Absolutely no late boarding is possible, so please be on time!
9:50pm Return to the docks

Note: Since this cruise is on the Tagus River, we expect it to be calm. However, if you are extremely prone to getting seasick, please take any necessary precautions. (Alison highly recommends Scopolamine patches.)
